University of Washington

first station

Univerisity of Washington is one of the oldest colleges in the U.S. The seattle campus is situtated on the shores of Union and Portage Bays, with views of the Cascade Rango to the east and the Olympic Mountains to the west

Spring is the best season to visit UW. There are sakura trees around the campus, especially on the Quad. Sit under the tree and enjoy the view, How fantastic it is!

Besides, the gothic architectures in the campus are special and unique. Suzzalo Library is the most famous building in campus, it is romantic and historical, like the castle in Henry Potter. Sit in this library, like you are learning majic in the film.
