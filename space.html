Space Needle

final station

At the end of the day, after vist University of Washington and Pike Place Market, we are going to our last station, space needle.

Space Needle is an observation tower in Seattle, a landmark of the Pacific Northwest, and an icon of Seattle.

Visitors can reach the top of the Space Needle by elevators in 41 seconds. At the top of the tower, visitors can see the beautiful view of Seattle in all direction!
